6. Interest Rates and Payouts

SureDeposit offers investors a fixed interest rate of 1.25% per month. Here are important details about interest rates and payouts:

  1. Fixed Rate:

    SureDeposit offers investors a competitive fixed interest rate of 1.25% per month. This translates to an annual interest rate capped at 15%. We understand the importance of stability in your investment returns. While we aim to maintain this rate consistently, please be aware that, in rare cases, we may consider adjusting this rate based on market conditions. Any such changes will be made at our discretion and communicated to our investors.

  2. Monthly Payouts:

    Investors can anticipate regular and dependable returns with SureDeposit. Interest earned on your investments is calculated and disbursed to your account on or before the 5th of each month. This ensures a predictable income stream for our investors. In case you encounter any discrepancies in your payouts, we encourage you to reach out to us promptly using your registered mobile number or email.

  3. Interest Calculations:

    Our interest calculations are designed to provide clarity and fairness to our investors. The calculation is based on the number of days your investment amount is present in your account within a given month. We follow a specific methodology for interest calculations based on the investment date:

    1. Upto 5 Days:

      For new amounts added within this timeframe, no interest is earned. However, investors will earn a full 1.25% interest on the previously invested amount, provided the previous investment was made at least 30 days prior.

    2. 6 Days - 25 Days:

      Investors will earn a partial interest on newly added amounts. This partial interest is calculated for the number of days the investment remains in the account during this period. If there is a previous investment, the previously invested amount will continue to fetch the full 1.25% interest along with the partial interest for the new amount.

    3. 25 Days+:

      For investments present in the account for 25 days or more within a month, investors will earn the full 1.25% interest on the entire invested amount.

  4. Flexible Tenure:

    SureDeposit offers flexibility when it comes to investment tenure. There is no fixed maturity period for your investments. You have the freedom to continue your investment until you decide to make a withdrawal. Your investment will continue to earn interest until the withdrawal is initiated.

  5. Reinvestment:

    To help you maximize your potential returns, SureDeposit offers investors the option to reinvest their earned interest. The reinvestment feature allows you to put your earnings back to work without any minimum amount requirement. This provides an opportunity for compounding your returns over time, ultimately enhancing your investment portfolio.

8. Withdrawals

As part of our commitment to providing a convenient and flexible investment platform, SureDeposit allows investors to withdraw their available balance at any time. We understand the importance of accessibility to your funds, and we've outlined the key details related to withdrawals for your convenience:

Withdrawal Types

  1. Withdraw Interest:

    This withdrawal option allows you to access the interest earned on your investments quickly. You can initiate this withdrawal at any time, providing you with the freedom to manage your earnings efficiently.

    Withdrawals under this option are typically processed swiftly, with funds usually credited within 48 hours. The minimum amount you can withdraw using this option is Rs. 100, ensuring accessibility even for smaller balances.

  2. Withdraw Investment:

    We understand that investment goals vary, and sometimes you may wish to access not only the interest but also a portion of your initial investment. SureDeposit offers two sub-options under the "Withdraw Investment" category:

    1. Quick Withdrawal:

      Quick Withdrawal is designed for those who seek a rapid access option to their invested capital. Similar to the Withdraw Interest option, you have the flexibility to initiate this withdrawal at any time.

      Quick Withdrawals are typically processed within 48 hours, providing a swift turnaround for your financial needs.

      The minimum amount for a Quick Withdrawal is Rs. 500. Please note that a nominal withdrawal fee of 1% may apply to Quick Withdrawals.

    2. Standard Withdrawal:

      Standard Withdrawal offers an alternative for investors looking for a predictable and scheduled withdrawal option. With this choice, your requested amount will be credited on the 5th of the following month, ensuring a consistent payout schedule.

      The amount will be credited to your account on the 5th of the next month if there is a minimum 30-day difference between the date of withdrawal and the 5th of the next month. However, if the time gap is less than 30 days, the withdrawal will be processed on the 5th of the next to next month.

      The minimum amount for a Quick Withdrawal is Rs. 500. There are no withdrawal fees associated with Standard Withdrawals, making it a cost-effective choice for planned access to your investments.

Other Withdrawal Terms

  1. Withdrawal Processing:

    At SureDeposit, we strive to ensure that your withdrawal requests are handled promptly and efficiently. Withdrawal processing times are typically within a reasonable timeframe. While we aim to make your funds available to you as quickly as possible, please note that the exact processing duration may vary depending on factors such as transaction volumes and security measures. Rest assured that we are dedicated to expediting the withdrawal process to meet your financial needs.

  2. Fees:

    We believe in transparency, and it's essential to be aware that there may be certain withdrawal restrictions in place. In some instances, SureDeposit reserves the right to charge nominal withdrawal fees (1%) associated with withdrawals. These fees, if applicable, are designed to cover the costs of transaction processing and security measures. Please refer to our fee schedule or contact our customer support team for specific details regarding withdrawal fees.

  3. Withdrawal Limits:

    To maintain the stability and liquidity of our platform and ensure that all investors can access their funds when needed, we may implement certain withdrawal limits. These limits are set to prevent any disruptions to the overall operation of the platform and to manage cash flow effectively. While these limits are in place, we work diligently to strike a balance between your convenience and the platform's operational needs. Rest assured that we continuously evaluate and adjust these limits as necessary to best serve our investors.

When you choose to withdraw a portion of your investment, please be aware that the withdrawn amount will not accrue any interest during the withdrawal process. Additionally, the withdrawn amount will not be factored into future interest calculations. This means that the withdrawn funds will not contribute to interest earnings while they are being processed for withdrawal.
