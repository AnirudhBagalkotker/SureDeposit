1. Introduction

SureDeposit is dedicated to providing a transparent and secure platform for peer-to-peer lending, where investors can grow their savings and borrowers can access fair and accessible loans. As part of our commitment to responsible financial services, we collect and process personal information only as necessary to provide our services and to fulfill legal and regulatory requirements.

We understand that you may have concerns about the collection and use of your data. This Privacy Policy is designed to address those concerns and provide you with a clear understanding of how we handle your information.

2. Information We Collect

SureDeposit collects various types of information to provide and improve our services. The information we collect falls into the following categories:

  1. Personal Information:

    To create and manage your account, we collect the following personal details:

    1. Full Name
    2. Address
    3. Date of Birth
    4. Contact Information (Email Address, Phone Number)
  2. Financial Information:

    To facilitate investments and withdrawals, we collect the following financial data:

    1. Bank Account Details
    2. Transaction History
  3. Identity Verification:

    In compliance with KYC (Know Your Customer) regulations, we may collect government-issued identification documents, such as:

    1. Passport
    2. Driver's License
    3. National ID
  4. Usage Data:

    We automatically collect information about your interactions with our platform, which may include:

    1. IP Addresses
    2. Device Information
    3. Browser Type
    4. Browsing History

3. How We Use Your Information

SureDeposit uses the information we collect for the following purposes:

  1. To Provide Services:

    Your personal and financial information is used to create and manage your account, process investments, and facilitate withdrawals.

  2. Risk Assessment:

    We use data, including credit history and identity verification, to assess the risk associated with borrowers and ensure responsible lending practices.

  3. Communications:

    We may contact you regarding account updates, important announcements, or marketing information, provided you have consented to such communications.

  4. Security:

    Your information is used to maintain the security and integrity of our platform, detect and prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.

4. Information Sharing

SureDeposit shares your information only as necessary to provide our services and as outlined in this Privacy Policy:

  1. Third-Party Service Providers:

    We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers to help us operate our platform and deliver services to you. These providers are contractually obligated to safeguard your data.

  2. Legal Requirements:

    We may disclose your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes.

  3. Business Transfers:

    In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity. You will be notified of such changes.

5. Security Measures

At SureDeposit, your security is paramount. We implement stringent security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Some of our security practices include:

  1. Encryption:

    We use encryption protocols to protect data during transmission.

  2. Access Control:

    Your data is stored securely and is accessible only to authorized personnel.

  3. Regular Audits:

    We conduct regular security audits to identify and mitigate vulnerabilities.

While we take every possible precaution to protect your data, it's essential to keep in mind that no online platform is entirely immune to risks. You should also take precautions to protect your account, such as using strong passwords and not sharing your login credentials.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services, fulfill legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You have the right to request the deletion of your account and data, subject to applicable laws.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

SureDeposit uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, monitor website performance, and analyze user behavior. Cookies are small pieces of data that are stored on your device when you visit a website. These technologies help us:

  1. Remember your preferences and settings.
  2. Analyze how you use our platform.
  3. Deliver personalized content and advertisements.

You can control your cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our platform.

8. Children's Privacy

SureDeposit services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect or store information from minors. If you believe that a minor has provided us with their personal information, please contact us, and we will take appropriate steps to address the issue.

9. Your Choices

You have certain rights and choices regarding your personal information:

  1. Access and Correction:

    You may access, correct, update, or delete your personal information through your account settings or by contacting us.

  2. Marketing Communications:

    You may opt-out of receiving marketing communications from us at any time.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

SureDeposit may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant changes. Your continued use of our platform after such modifications constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us. We value your privacy and will respond promptly to address your inquiries.
