FAQs

Got a question? We've got you covered! Check out some of the most commonly asked questions about us.

Can I withdraw my investment before the term ends?

Yes, you can withdraw your investment amount at any time, although you may incur a charge of 1% for instant withdrawals
When will I receive my investment back if I withdraw?

If withdrawal is made before the last day of the month, you will receive your investment on 5th of the next month in your bank account.
What is Quick Withdrawal?

You can also receive your funds within 48 hours of your request with Instant Withdraw feature.
How is interest calculated on my investment ?

Interests on your deposit are calculated on the basis of number of days for which you have invested.

Can I withdraw my interest anytime?

Yes, with SureDeposit, you have flexible deposit and withdrawal options, which means you can withdraw your interest anytime you want.
How often are interest payouts made?

Interest payouts are made on a monthly basis directly to your account.

Why am I Wait-Listed for a Loan?

Based on your credit history, SureDeposit issues loan only to those who fall in the eligible age & CIBIL score criteria.
How can I be eligible for a Loan?

Build your credibility by increasing activity on SureDeposit and soon you will be eligible too!
How safe is my investment with SureDeposit?

At SureDeposit, we take the security of your investment seriously. You can be sure in our commitment to securing your assets.
What are the benefits of investing with SureDeposit?

With SureDeposit, experience high interest rates on savings, flexible deposit and withdrawal options, within a safe and secure investment platform.
What happens if money gets deducted from my bank account but balance doesn't show in SureDeposit?

In that case, either the amount will be added to SureDeposit within 12 hours or if payment fails, it will be refunded to the origin bank account within 7 to 10 working days.
What are the rewards I can get?

Receive a ₹50 Joining Bonus, and earn ₹100 reward to refer a friend. Your friend will enjoy additional ₹50 as referral reward upon joining.
How can I withdraw my rewards?

To withdraw your rewards, navigate to the Rewards section and select the Withdraw button. You can withdraw your rewards once the balance exceeds ₹250.

When can I withdraw my rewards?

You can withdraw your rewards once your balance reaches ₹250 or more. To increase your rewards, refer friends and earn ₹100 for each successful referral.
I am not able to withdraw my rewards?

To withdraw your rewards, ensure that you and the friends you referred have made an investment with SureDeposit.
